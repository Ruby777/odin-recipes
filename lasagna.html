Lasagna Recipe

Description

When our chef submitted this lasagna recipe, we had no idea how successful it would become

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Garlic and Onion
  • Tomato paste
  • Crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Sugar
  • Italian spice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Parmesan
  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to layer Lasagna

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan

How long to cook Lasagna

The assembled lasagna should take about 50minutes to cook in an oven preheated to 375 degrees F. Cover it with foil for the first 25 minutes, then let it cook uncovered for the final 25 minutes. Also, it's important to let the lasagna rest at room temperature for about 15 minutes before you cut into it.
